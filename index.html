TERMS AND CONDITIONS

All fees to be paid at the office & at the time of admission. Fees paid will not be refunded under any circumstances.

Candidate has to confirm 15 days prior for R.T.O. Test only on Fridays & Reporting Time is 8.00 a.m. on Test Ground. Fine of Rs. 250/- will be levied before next appointment.

Irregular attendance, negligence & frequent absent will be considered as a serious breach of discipline & their training may be stopped.

The candidate remaining absent from school without prior permission in writing for 10 days or more will be tested for his experience in driving & will be charged extra fees accordingly.

Course will be conducted as per the rules mentioned in Motor Vehicle Act. & as per PRAVIN DRIVING SCHOOL Code of Conduct.

No person will be allowed to accompany candidate at the time of driving sessions.
